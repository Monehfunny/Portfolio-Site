ALTERED STATE

[Coming Q4 2025 - Q1 2026]

Altered State is a Roblox Mandela Catalogue inspired game, it plays out very similary to FNAF (Five Nights at Freddy's).

The Player(s) will have to watch over a house throughout the night, using security cameras, windows, and flashlights to their survival. There is different types of alternates, and they can break it in through windows, doors, etc... To prevent an alternate you have to shut the windows and hold it until they go away (similar to doors in FNAF 4).
